P1 Data We Collect 我们收集的数据

We do not directly collect personal data. No account registration is required. Your books, reading progress, bookmarks, and settings are stored only on your device using local storage and are never transmitted to our servers.

However, third-party SDKs integrated into the app (Google AdMob, RevenueCat, Expo) may collect certain data as described in Section P2. The data types collected indirectly through these SDKs include:

  • Device advertising identifier (IDFA on iOS, GAID on Android) — collected by Google AdMob for ad delivery
  • Anonymous app user ID — generated by RevenueCat to manage subscription state; not linked to your real identity
  • Purchase and subscription history — processed by RevenueCat via App Store / Google Play
  • Device type, OS version, app version — used by RevenueCat and Expo for compatibility and update delivery
  • Anonymous ad interaction data (impressions, clicks) — collected by Google AdMob

我们不直接收集个人数据。无需注册账号。您的书籍、阅读进度、书签和设置仅通过本地存储保存在您的设备上，不会传输至我们的服务器。

  • 设备广告标识符（iOS 上的 IDFA、Android 上的 GAID）——由 Google AdMob 用于广告投放
  • 匿名应用用户 ID——由 RevenueCat 生成用于管理订阅状态，不与您的真实身份关联
  • 购买和订阅历史——由 RevenueCat 通过 App Store / Google Play 处理

P3 Advertising Identifiers & ATT 广告标识符与 ATT

InkVoice serves non-personalized ads only. We have configured Google AdMob to request non-personalized advertisements, which limits data collection compared to personalized ad serving.

iOS — App Tracking Transparency (ATT): On iOS 14.5 and later, Apple requires apps to request permission before accessing the IDFA (Identifier for Advertisers). If the app presents an ATT prompt, you may choose to allow or deny tracking. Regardless of your choice, InkVoice will continue to show non-personalized ads.

Android: Google Advertising ID (GAID) may be used by AdMob for non-personalized ad delivery. You can reset or opt out of your GAID in Android device settings under Google > Ads.

InkVoice 墨声仅投放非个性化广告。我们已将 Google AdMob 配置为请求非个性化广告，与个性化广告相比，数据收集更为有限。

iOS——App Tracking Transparency（ATT）：在 iOS 14.5 及以上系统中，Apple 要求应用在访问 IDFA（广告主标识符）前须获得用户许可。若应用弹出 ATT 授权提示，您可选择允许或拒绝。无论您的选择如何，InkVoice 墨声都将继续展示非个性化广告。

Android：Google 广告 ID（GAID）可能被 AdMob 用于非个性化广告投放。您可在 Android 设备设置的"Google > 广告"中重置或选择退出 GAID。
